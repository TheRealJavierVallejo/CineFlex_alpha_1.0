## üåü New Studio Features

### 1. Hollywood-Grade Interface

- **Left Panel (Controls)**: Tabbed interface for Description, Camera Settings, and Cast selection.
- **Right Panel (Canvas)**: A massive, real-time preview area to see your AI generations in high detail.

- Organize your movie linearly with Scenes and Shot Strips.
- Write dialogue and action notes directly in the timeline.

## üöÄ Quick Start

1. **Create Project**: Launch the app and click "Create New".
2. **Take the Tour**: Follow the onboarding guide to learn the interface.
3. **Add Scenes**: Go to the **Timeline** tab and add your first scene header.
4. **Create Shots**: Click the blue "**+ NEW SHOT**" button in the top right.
5. **Generate**: Inside the Shot Editor, describe your vision and click **RENDER SHOT**.
